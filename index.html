D3Times

In this documentation, we utilize d3 to visualize education across the states against HIV Testing. Within the United States, as of 2013, there are 1.2 Million individuals living with HIV. The best way to treat and prevent HIV is to be tested and enter treatment. It is estimated that one in seven indivudals in america are unaware of their status. The goal of this visualization is to demonstrate that education and testing are correlated.
